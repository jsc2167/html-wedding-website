Donating to Charities

We are fortunate enough to have everything we need. If you would like to give a gift, we would like to request that you donate to one of our favorite charities in our names instead. All of our chosen charities have been recognized as charities that use their donations incredibly effectively.

Union of Concerned Scientists

The Union of Concerned Scientists focuses on applying scientific research and methods to develop and implement innovative, practical solutions to the Earth's most pressing problems, from slowing climate change to developing sustainible agriculture and transportation.
